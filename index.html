For Seniors

This is a platform which is designed for you. Find activities you like, meet other people and share your stories with them. We help you on the way and take care of your transport! Book our pick-up service for each event directly on our platform.

For Family members

Are you a family member and want to help your loved one to enjoy their time? You can use Goldies for another person and book activities for them. When you sign up, indicate that you are a family member. We take care of the transport for all activities!

For Caregiver

Are you a caregiver in a nursing home? You can use Goldies to arrange activities for residents in your home. When you sign up, indicate that you are a caregiver. We take care of the transport for all activities!

Choose the transport
that fits your needs

Would you like to leave your house, but feel unsteady on your feet? No problem with Goldies! With each event you can choose your preferred transport mode and get picked up right on your door steps.

We offer a selection of transport modes with every activity you book on our platform.

But because we care for our environment at Goldies, we encourage you to try our Cargo Bike and get some wind in your hair!
