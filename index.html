We connect likeminded people and fight loneliness among seniors

Did you know socialising can strengthen your immune system? Studies showed that friendships improve physical and mental health.

There are tons of things you can do, not matter how old you are! We are here to help you making your life enjoyable.

Would you like to leave your house, but feel unsteady on your feet? No problem with Goldies! With each event you can choose your preferred transport mode and get picked up right on your door steps.

What do clients say about Goldies?

"

I missed spending time with likeminded people. When you’re old, everything is about sacrifices and health problems. With Goldies I can just have a good time without worrying about anything! They pick me up, they bring me home and I can just enjoy being outside with other people.

Horst, retired, 82 years old

"

After my husband passed away I stopped doing the things I used to love. Then my daughter discovered Goldies and booked me into a drawing class. I met some interesting people. Some of them are artists and a lot younger than me, they remind me of how my husband was when he was a young artist. It's so refreshing, thanks Goldies!

Gertrud, retired, 84 years old
