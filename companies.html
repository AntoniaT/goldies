How we work with event organizers

Why become an event organiser?

Do you organise events or activities for seniors? Maybe you work in an organisation that offers activities for elderly people, but you don’t know how to reach more seniors that could benefit from your activities? That’s where we can help! We are the biggest platform for seniors in Denmark and we know how to reach your target audience!

We handle booking and transport

With our intuitive dashboard, you get an overview of all incoming bookings. To make sure all senior citizens regardless of their impairments can enjoy your activities, we included a transport service to our platform. We pick seniors up at their homes and bring them to your event. We also bring them home after.

How does it work for your organisation?

In order to become one of our event organisers, you have to sign up on our website. After you answer our questions via email, we visit your company and have a quick chat. Once you are verified by our specialists, you can immediately set your events live, which can then be seen and booked by thousands of customers.

Answers to your questions

You most definitely can, yes! We are here to help your organisation reach the right people, not matter if you run on volunteer work or have a profitable business. Our goal is to connect seniors with activities they enjoy and if you facilitate activities like that we would love to have you on board.
You can change an event on your dashboard up to 48 hours before the event would start. However, if you really need to change it later than 48 hours before the event, please call our customer service at +45 500 600 01 and we will help you arrange that. If you have to change an event up to 48 hours beforehand, log in to your dashboard first. Then go to the menu button "your events" on the left menu bar. Choose the event you want to change and change the parameter you need to adapt. Click on "publish my changes" and your event will be updated automatically. Please note that we give all participants notice of the changes once you publish them. Therefore make sure that the changes are correct before you publish them. You can also save the changes as a draft first, without publishing if you are not sure.
Yes, you can see how many seniors signed up to your event. Just go to the left menu bar and click the button "my events". Then choose the event you want and click it. You will then see all the details for your event and the list of people who already signed up for your event.
If you have to cancel an event more than 48hours before it will start, you can do that on your dashboard. Just go to the left menu bar and click the button "my events". Then choose the event you want to cancel and click it. You will find a red button that says "cancel the event". Click the button and confirm that you have to cancel. If you want, you can send a message to tell your customers why you have to cancel the event, they will appreciate that. The customers will then be informed about the cancellation automatically. However, if you need to cancel an event less than 48hours before the start you have to call our customer service at +45 80 600 20. We will handle the cancellation for you.
