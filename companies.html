How we work with event organizers

Why become an event organiser?

Do you organise events or activities for seniors? Maybe you work in an organisation that offers activities for elderly people, but you don’t know how to reach more seniors that could benefit from your activities? That’s where we can help! We are the biggest platform for seniors in Denmark and we know how to reach your target audience!

We handle booking and transport

With our intuitive dashboard, you get an overview of all incoming bookings. To make sure all senior citizens regardless of their impairments can enjoy your activities, we included a transport service to our platform. We pick seniors up at their homes and bring them to your event. We also bring them home after.

How does it work for your organisation?

In order to become one of our event organisers, you have to sign up on our website. After you answer our questions via email, we visit your company and have a quick chat. Once you are verified by our specialists, you can immediately set your events live, which can then be seen and booked by thousands of customers.
