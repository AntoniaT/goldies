How does it work?

Our application is designed to make searching for an event and participating in activities simple and easy! However, if you need some help, we’re here for you.

Join an event

Simply open up the platform and choose the event you like. Click the join button to register for the event.

Get picked up

After you join the event you can choose your preferred transport mode. A driver will pick you up at your door steps.

Have fun!

Now you can enjoy the event and connect with other people. And don't worry, we'll bring you home afterwards.

Answers to your questions

When you sign up, make sure to indicate that you need personal assistance. Whenever you click "join" to attend an activity you will be able to choose personal assistance if we offer it for this particular event. We usually do, but sometimes it is not possible for us to offer that kind of service for specific events. If in doubt, you can always call our customer service at +45 80 600 20, we are happy to assist.
You most definitely can! When signing up for Goldies, you will be prompted questions about your mobility. Please make sure to indicate that you are in a wheelchair, so we can suggest the best activities for you. We will facilitate transport for you with our special wheelchair accessible taxis.
Yes we will contact you one hour before the driver will pick you up to make sure you are ready. We also send you notifications two and one day before the event will start. When you sign up on the platform, you will be asked how you want to be contacted. You can choose between a phone call, an sms or just a notification inside the application. You do not have to be able to read text messages or messages inside the application, if you choose to be contacted via a phone call.
Inside Goldies, you can go to your calender and choose the event you have to cancel. If you click on it, you will see a button saying "cancel". Just click the button and confirm that you cannot make it to this event. We will automatically receive your cancellation and tell the organizer. That is all you have to do. We hope you will join another time!

You can use the platform together with a senior. When you sign up, simply choose the option to sign up as family member. Inside the platform you will see that your account differs in colour to the senior's account and in the top right corner you will see your profile with your families name.
